AI and Machine Learning







AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the broad concept of enabling machines to perform human-like cognitive functions, while Machine Learning (ML) isa subset of AI that uses algorithms and statistical models to allow systems to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed. In essence, AI is the goal of creating intelligent machines, and ML is one of the primary methods for achieving it.
