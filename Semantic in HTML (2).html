Facts about Shinchan

Shinchan is a funny cartoon which is equally popular among kids, teens and young people. It's main character is a five year old naughty boy named Shinchan. It is a Japanese cartoon available in japanese, hindi, bengali etc.

It is said that there is a sad real story behind making this cartoon. Shinchan is a real character. One day he went for shopping with his mother and younger sister, Himawari. When his mother was busy in buying things, himawari went to the middle of a street following a ball. Shinchan also went there to save her but suddenly a car came in a rush and crashed both of them. Then his mother wrote the stories and commited suicide for her guilt.
