Hobbies

Travelling

Travelling is a great hobby which allows us to embrace our inner-self along with exploring new places.

Art & Craft

Art & Craft are hands-on activities that involve creating objects, often for personal enjoyment, expression, or for a functional purpose.

Photography

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating images by recording light with a camera, either chemically on film or electronically with a sensor.

Gardening

Gardening is the practice of cultivating and tending plants in a designated area to grow various produce like vegetables, fruits, and flowers, for aesthetic enjoyment, food production, or other uses.

Music

Music can be a wonderful hobby, whether you enjoy actively listening to music and building collections, or you create music by playing an instrument, singing, or producing.
